Illustrations, Logos, Icons, and Doodles

A small collection of illustrations I've made for friends and clients

Digital illustration, and in particular icon design, helps pull a product together. The little details that help connect great UI, UX, Branding, and feature requirements are the cherry on top of my job.

In this collection are a few logos I've made for friends and family. One I am particularly proud of is the College Basketball Subreddit logo (currently in use for all r/cbb branding and web materials). This logo was one of the more challenging I've made because it needed to be usable even at very small sizes. The subreddit currently has over 1 million users.

I have also included a sample from an illustrative icon set I made for a friend's personal project. The icon set needed to be more attention grabbing than simple filled shapes, but not so complicated that it drew away from the text on the page.

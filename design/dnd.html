Dungeons & Dragons Character Creation App

Trying my hand at designing a product from the ground up

As a personal challenge, I have begun designing a mobile app to help turn the Dungeons & Dragons character creation process into a delightful, efficient experience. I've been playing the game my whole life, and while I love coming up with a unique, magical person to play, the actual character creation process that comes with the game is exhausting.

I began interviewing friends and family on their experiences making characters. I interviewed 10 people from all walks of life (though 3 personas emerged) and across the board I found that players consistently have difficulty with D&D's character creation system.

I compiled all the interview results into bite-sized statements, or sentiments, and sorted them by theme. I found that the largest areas for improvement are (1) players want to know how the rest of the group is making choices, (2) there is too much content to read to get the process done quickly, and (3) the mechanics of the game are confusing to new players and it causes character creation to feel opaque.

After that I decided to map my main persona's user journey using the common sentiments I found earlier. Plotting her positive and negative emotions throughout the journey helped me start to see how a product could ease the process and actually turn character creation into a game in and of itself. This realization lead me to start thinking about the product in terms of game design, and when ideating I settled on a Jackbox-inspired, group-oriented, creation game using your mobile device and a monitor.

At this stage, I have mapped out the full product flow and am starting to wireframe the nitty gritty mechanics of the game. I have a lot more ideation and validating to do, but I have absolutely loved thinking about a product purely from a user experience point of view.
